About us

The Public Participation Forum focuses on enhancing public participation in governance,as outlined by the Constitution of Kenya 2010. It introduces a streamlined approach for submitting views on laws under consideration by the Kenyan Parliament, as well as on government initiatives or policies that require public input. By providing comprehensive information, the aim is to ensure that all voices are heard in decisions made by the Legislature, Executive, Judiciary and other government bodies..
